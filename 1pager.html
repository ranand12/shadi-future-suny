The Gap

SUNY has rich data across 64 campuses — 3 SIS vendors, unified Brightspace LMS, SIRIS/SUNY BI warehouse with 45+ reports/campus/year. But:

No system-wide predictive analytics platform. Advisors log into 4-5 separate systems with no unified student view. Students are flagged only after they've already disengaged. The data exists — the AI layer does not.
