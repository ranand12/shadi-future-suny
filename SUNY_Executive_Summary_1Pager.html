The Gap: Fragmented Data & Why Prediction Alone Fails

SUNY Broome case study: 25% headcount decline, 47.9% retention rate, 250 students per advisor, 45% with DFW grades. No unified Student 360 view across 64 campuses — data siloed in SIS, LMS, financial aid, and advising systems with campus-by-campus vendor procurement.

Critical insight: A DOE-funded RCT at 11 universities found predictive analytics alone produced no significant improvements — except at Georgia State, which had pre-existing data infrastructure. ASAP|ACE has the intervention infrastructure. It lacks the data/AI layer. Google Cloud fills this gap.
